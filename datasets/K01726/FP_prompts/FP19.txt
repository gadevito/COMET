MTC: US3.006– Accept a teleconsultation invitation
Description: The actor accepts an invitation for a “requested” teleconsultation. 
Actors: Specialist.
Precondition
There is a “requested” teleconsultation. 
Main scenario
1.	The actor clicks the “Accept teleconsultation invitation” relating to the teleconsultation of his (her) interest. 
2.	The system updates the teleconsultation invitation as “accepted” and saves the teleconsultation event on the actor’s agenda.
