MTC: US4.001– Start a televisit/teleconsultation
Description: The actor starts the televisit and (or) the teleconsultation on the current date and time.
Actors: Specialist, Family doctor.
Precondition
A teleconsultation (or televisit) event is scheduled on the current date and time.
Main scenario
1.	The actor clicks the “Start televisit/teleconsultation” button relating the televisit/teleconsultation of his (or her) interest.
2.	The system sends the request to the XXX system to start the meeting.
